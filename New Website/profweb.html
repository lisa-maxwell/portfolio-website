Keep It Lean

Scrum Fundamentals Certified

Efficiency and productivity sparks joy. That's why I'm Scrum Fundamentals Certified (certificate ID # 694372) and use the Agile framework. One of my greatest achievements during my time in the public school system was leading the Multi-Tiered System Of Support (MTSS) Team for a Title I school with over 900 students during the 18-19 school year. I restructured the team and rebuilt in an Agile framework. With my leadership, the team implemented daily standup meetings and began the practice of collecting qualitative and quantitative data on an incremental schedule. I also designed and built a data processing platform in Excel for the team to use to report individual student data and identify trends in performance. The data speaks for itself: 28% more students were referred to the MTSS framework to receive the essential accommodations they needed to be successful.
